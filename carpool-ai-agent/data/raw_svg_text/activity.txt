Activity Diagram - Complete Ride Lifecycle

START
   ↓
[User Search for Ride]
   ↓
{Is ride available?}
   ├─NO→ [Show "No rides available"]
   │       ↓
   │      END
   │
   └─YES→ [Request Ride from Driver]
           ↓
           {Driver Response Timeout?}
           ├─YES (30s)→ [Request expires]
           │             ↓
           │            [Notify user]
           │             ↓
           │            END
           │
           └─NO→ [Driver decides]
                  ↓
                  {Accept Ride?}
                  ├─NO→ [Decline notification sent]
                  │      ↓
                  │     [Suggest alternative rides]
                  │      ↓
                  │     END
                  │
                  └─YES→ [Ride Confirmed]
                          ↓
                          [Send confirmation to both]
                          ↓
                          {Schedule parallel:}
                          ├─ [Track driver location]
                          ├─ [Calculate ETA]
                          ├─ [Prepare payment method]
                          └─ [Send pre-ride notifications]
                          ↓
                          [User & Driver at pickup location]
                          ↓
                          {Is driver on time?}
                          ├─NO (>10min)→ [Trigger cancellation flow]
                          │               ↓
                          │              [Activate backup driver]
                          │               ↓
                          │              [Notify original user]
                          │               ↓
                          │              BACKUP_FLOW
                          │
                          └─YES→ [Start ride]
                                  ↓
                                  [Begin tracking route]
                                  ↓
                                  {Is route optimal?}
                                  ├─NO→ [Suggest route adjustment]
                                  │
                                  └─YES→ [Continue ride]
                                          ↓
                                          [Intermediate stops if any]
                                          ↓
                                          [Reach destination]
                                          ↓
                                          {Ride completed?}
                                          ├─NO→ [Extended ride handling]
                                          │
                                          └─YES→ [End ride]
                                                  ↓
                                                  [Calculate cost]
                                                  ↓
                                                  [Process payment]
                                                  ↓
                                                  {Payment successful?}
                                                  ├─NO→ [Retry payment]
                                                  │      ↓
                                                  │     {Max retries exceeded?}
                                                  │     ├─YES→ [Manual review]
                                                  │     └─NO→ [Continue retry]
                                                  │
                                                  └─YES→ [Send receipt]
                                                          ↓
                                                          [Request ratings]
                                                          ↓
                                                          {Both rated?}
                                                          ├─NO→ [Send reminder]
                                                          │      ↓
                                                          │     [Archive ride]
                                                          │
                                                          └─YES→ [Update ratings]
                                                                  ↓
                                                                  [Calculate CO2 savings]
                                                                  ↓
                                                                  [Add to user stats]
                                                                  ↓
                                                                  [Send thank you message]
                                                                  ↓
                                                                  [Archive ride in history]
                                                                  ↓
                                                                  END

BACKUP_FLOW:
   ↓
[Trigger backup driver matching]
   ↓
[Query backup drivers in proximity]
   ↓
{Backup drivers available?}
   ├─NO→ [Offer ride credits/refund]
   │      ↓
   │     END
   │
   └─YES→ [AI rank backup drivers]
           ↓
           [Send request to top match]
           ↓
           {Backup accepts?}
           ├─NO→ [Try next backup]
           │      ↓
           │     {More backups?}
           │     ├─NO→ [Offer refund]
           │     └─YES→ Back to "Send request"
           │
           └─YES→ [Reassign ride to backup]
                   ↓
                   [Update ride metadata]
                   ↓
                   [Notify original user]
                   ↓
                   Continue main flow from [Start ride]
